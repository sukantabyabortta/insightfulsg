About Us

Your Trusted Partner for
Financial Excellence & Business Growth

At Insightful Business Consultant Pte. Ltd., we are committed to helping businesses thrive through expert accounting, taxation, compliance, and strategic advisory services. With over a decade of professional experience, we partner with startups, SMEs, and growing enterprises to simplify financial management, ensure regulatory compliance, and support informed business decisions.

Free up your time while we keep your financial records accurate, compliant, and ready for informed business decisions. Our experienced professionals work as an extension of your team, delivering practical solutions tailored to your business goals, so you can focus on what matters most—growing your business.

We believe that successful businesses are built on financial clarity, operational efficiency, and strategic planning. By combining industry expertise, analytical thinking, and a client-first approach, we help organisations navigate complex financial and regulatory environments with confidence.

At Insightful Business Consultant Pte. Ltd., our commitment goes beyond compliance. We strive to build long-term partnerships founded on integrity, transparency, and excellence, empowering our clients with the insights and support they need to achieve sustainable growth.

“Simplifying Finance. Strengthening Businesses. Inspiring Growth.”

Our Team

Meet Our Professionals

The experienced team behind every insight, strategy, and solution we deliver.

Founder & CEO

Anubha Jain

Anubha Jain is the Founder and CEO of Insightful Business Consultants Pte. Ltd. The company offers one stop solution for incorporation, corporate secretarial services , Accounting , Tax advisory and planning and compilation.

Anubha graduated from St. Xavier’s College, Kolkata with a B.Com (hons) in 2010. She is a member of ICAI (The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India) , ISCA (Institute of Singapore Chartered Accountants) and CSIS (Chartered Secretary Institute of Singapore).

Anubha has over 10 years of experience in handling clients and providing bespoke compliance services.

Finance & Compliance Professional

Dipika Chitlangia

13 years. Three industries. One purpose — transforming finance from a reporting function into a strategic business enabler.

With over 13 years of experience across agri-business, manpower services, and professional accounting practice, Dipika has built expertise in financial reporting, management accounting, budgeting, internal controls, payroll administration, compliance, and business operations. Her strong analytical background enables her to look beyond the numbers, helping businesses make informed decisions with confidence.

Holding a Postgraduate Certification in Business Analytics from IIT Roorkee, she combines financial expertise with data-driven insights to support business growth, operational efficiency, and sound financial management.

Throughout her career, Dipika has managed end-to-end finance operations, including month-end closing, financial reporting, budgeting, payroll administration, statutory compliance, and audit coordination. She has successfully led finance teams, managed payroll for large workforces, and consistently delivered accurate financial reporting while maintaining high standards of governance and operational excellence.

At Insightful Business Consultant Pte. Ltd., she works closely with clients to strengthen financial processes, improve operational efficiency, and provide reliable accounting and compliance support that enables business owners to focus on growth.
