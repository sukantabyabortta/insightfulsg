Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common questions about our company incorporation, corporate compliance, accounting, taxation, payroll, and business advisory services in Singapore.

What services does Insightful Business Consultant provide?

We provide end-to-end business support services, including company incorporation, corporate secretarial services, bookkeeping, accounting, taxation, GST compliance, payroll management, and business advisory solutions for startups, SMEs, and growing businesses.

Can you help me register a company in Singapore?

Yes. We assist with the complete company incorporation process, including business name reservation, preparation of incorporation documents, statutory filings, and post-incorporation compliance.

What information is required to incorporate a company in Singapore?

The incorporation process typically requires details of the proposed company name, business activities, shareholders, directors, company secretary, registered office address, and other statutory information. Our team will guide you through each step and ensure all requirements are met.

How long does it take to incorporate a company in Singapore?

Once all the required information and documents are received, most companies can be incorporated within 1–2 business days, subject to approval by ACRA.

Can foreign entrepreneurs register a company in Singapore?

Yes. We assist both local and foreign entrepreneurs with company incorporation and provide guidance on the regulatory requirements applicable to foreign-owned businesses.

What happens after my company is incorporated?

After incorporation, businesses must meet various statutory and tax compliance requirements. We provide ongoing support through corporate secretarial services, bookkeeping, payroll, GST compliance, tax filing, and annual regulatory filings to help your business remain compliant.

Do you provide corporate secretarial services?

Yes. We offer comprehensive corporate secretarial services, including maintaining statutory registers, preparing board and shareholder resolutions, filing annual returns, and ensuring ongoing compliance with ACRA regulations.

Do you provide GST and corporate tax filing services?

Yes. We assist with GST registration and filing, Estimated Chargeable Income (ECI), corporate income tax returns, personal income tax, and ongoing IRAS compliance.

Can you manage our bookkeeping and payroll?

Yes. Our bookkeeping and payroll services include transaction recording, bank reconciliation, financial reporting, payroll processing, CPF submissions, payslip preparation, and year-end reporting.

Do you work with startups and SMEs?

Absolutely. We specialise in supporting startups, SMEs, and growing businesses with practical, cost-effective solutions tailored to their unique business needs.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing depends on the scope of services and the complexity of your business. We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden charges. Contact us for a customised quotation.

Why should I choose Insightful Business Consultant?

We combine professional expertise with personalised service to deliver practical, reliable, and timely solutions. From company incorporation to ongoing compliance and business advisory, we are committed to helping businesses build a strong foundation and achieve sustainable growth.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple. Contact us through our website, email, or phone to schedule an initial consultation. We’ll understand your business needs and recommend the most suitable solutions to support your business journey.
